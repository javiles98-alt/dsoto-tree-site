About Us

DSoto Tree & Arborist Services is a family-owned and operated company that’s been serving the community for over 20 years. What started as a small operation with a single truck has grown into a trusted name across the region, thanks to our hard work, honest pricing, and commitment to excellent customer service. We’ve built a strong reputation in our hometown and nearby towns for being reliable, fair, and professional. Whether it’s trimming overgrown branches, removing dangerous trees, grinding stumps, or responding to storm damage, we bring decades of hands-on experience and care to every job. What sets us apart isn’t just what we do — it’s how we do it. We treat every property like it’s our own, making sure the job is done safely, thoroughly, and with complete respect for our customers’ time and space. That’s why so many of our clients come back to us and refer us to friends and family. At D.Soto Tree Services, we believe in doing the job right the first time — with honesty, pride, and a smile. We’re here when you need us, ready to help with all your tree service needs.
